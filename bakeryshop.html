!!BAKERY!!


A Bakery is an establishment that produces and sells flour-based food baked in an oven such as bread,cookies,cakes,pastries and pies. A place where cakes and breads are made or sold.

!!CAKES!!


cake is a sweet form of dessert that typically is that is baked in its oldest forms.cakes were modifications of breads. but cakes now covers a wide range of preparetions that can be simple or elaborate, and that shares features with other desserts such as pastries,meringues,custurds and pies.

!!BUNS!!


A Bun is a small, smoetimes sweet bread or bread roll, though they come in many shape and size. they are most commonly hand-sized or smaller, with a round top and flat bottom. Buns are usually made from flour,sugar,milk,yeast and butter.

!!PASTRY!!


Pastry is a dough of flour, water and shortening that maybe savoury or sweetened. word "PASRTIES" suggests many kinds of baked products from ingredients such as flour, sugur, milk, butter, shortening, baking powder and eggs.small tarts and oher sweet baked products are called pastries.

!!DONUTS!!
